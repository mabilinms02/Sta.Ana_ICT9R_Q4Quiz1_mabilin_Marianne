CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin (Crisostomo Ibarra)
  • Son of Don Rafael Ibarra
  • Childhood friends with Maria Clara
  • Studied in Europe for 7 years and went home to Philippines Nakapagaral sa Europa at ng pitong(7) taon ay bumalik ng Pilipinas
  • He is cultured and well-respected
María Clara de los Santos y Alba (Maria Clara)
  • Capitan Tiago and Pia Alba's daughter and the goddaughter of Friar Damaso
  • A well-mannered and graceful Roman Catholic
  • Went to Sta. Clara to study after Crisostomo left to Europe
Don Santiago De los Santos (Capitan Tiago)
  • A wealthy respected Filipino elite
  • Pia Alba's husband and Maria Clara's father
Doña Pia Alba
  • Born in a wealthy family in Sta. Cruz, Manila
  • Merried to Capitan Tigao and Maria Clara's mother
Isabel de los Santos (Tiya Isabel)
  • Aunt and primary caretaker of Maria Clara
  • Cousin of Capitan Tiago
Rafael Ibarra
  • Richest man in San Diego also the most virtuous and generous
  • Crisostomo Ibarra's father
Miguel Guevarra (Teniente Guevarra)
  • Elder lieutenant of the Guardia Civil
  • A close friend of and holds a deep respect for Don Rafael Ibarra
Narcisa (Sisa)
  • Settled and married in San Diego with an abusive, lazy gambler of a husband
  • Has two sons- Basilio and Crispin
Basilio
  • An optimistic and hopeful 10 year old child, demonstrating a certain maturity in despise to his father
  • The eldest son of Sisa and brother of Crispin.
Crispin
  • An innocent 7 year old sacristan of the church alongside his brother
  • The youngest son of Sisa and brother of Basilio
